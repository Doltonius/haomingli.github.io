--- layout: default title: The Night of Sevens ---

The cruel, amorous flow of time pours itself into
The mountains and seas that separate us.
How sublime, the static tides of emeralds and
The horizontal cliffs of sapphires!
How sad, all but obstacles they are for the tiny pearls
I sent on the way to you!

Yet be they pearls, the softness of their sheen lures the night
Into their warm embrace, my longing gaze; mingled into
The stream of Sevens, it permeates the dense rounds of
Erratic dances of symbols that circle your fatigued soul.
When our eyes meet, they whisper to each other silently:
Carry on, as my love has always and will forever.
